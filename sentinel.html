Sentinel

Sentinel is an animated illustration exploring themes of watchfulness and protection. This dynamic piece features a guardian figure overseeing its domain with silent vigilance.

Through subtle animation and atmospheric elements, this work creates a sense of both security and mystery, inviting viewers to contemplate the nature of observation and protection.
