Summer Camp

This illustration captures the vibrant and carefree spirit of summer camp experiences. The artwork depicts the joy of outdoor activities, friendship, and adventure that characterize these formative summer gatherings.

Through a bright color palette and energetic composition, the piece evokes nostalgia for childhood summers while celebrating the timeless appeal of communal outdoor experiences and the natural world.
